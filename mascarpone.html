CREMA MASCARPONE

This cream is a traditional Italian dessert originating from the Veneto region. Typically combined with Tiramisù, this cream can also be served on its own with some cookies on the side. Enjoy!
If you would like a video guide, here it is.

Ingredients and Special Materials Needed

Mascarpone cheese 500g
Eggs 3
Sugar 10 spoons
Electric mixer (shop here) N/A

Preparation Steps

  1. Separate the yolks from the egg whites. Place them in two separate bowls.
  2. Use the mixer to mix the yolks and sugar. Stop when you get a creamy mix.
  3. Add the mascarpone cheese and mix.
  4. On a separate bowl, mix the egg whites at high speed until the they look foamy.
  5. Add the foamy egg whites to the mixture and mix with a big spoon with circular movements.
  6. Store in the fridge for one hour.
  7. Your mascarpone cream is now ready to serve. Enjoy!
