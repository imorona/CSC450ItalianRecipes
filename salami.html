CHOCOLATE SALAMI

This salami is a traditional Italian dessert originating from the Sicily region. It is called salami because of its resemblance to the typical Italian meat salami, do not worry - this is a dessert. You can serve it with coffee, milk, or even just my itself!
If you would like a video guide, click here.

Ingredients and Special Materials Needed

Butter 100g
Eggs 2
Dry cookies 300g
Cacao powder N/A
Aluminum foil N/A
Electric mixer (shop here) N/A

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix the butter, eggs and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the caco powder to the mix until you get a brown creamy mix.
  3. Break the dry cookies.
  4. Add the pieces of dry cookies to the bowl and mix.
  5. Cut a piece of aluminum foil and flatten it.
  6. Add the mix (scoop by scoop) on the foil to form the shape of a salami.
  7. Wrap the salami and store in fridge for three hours.
  8. Repeat with the remaining mix.
  9. Your chocolate salami is now ready to serve. Enjoy!
